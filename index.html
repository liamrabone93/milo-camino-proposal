The Opportunity
The numbers that run Camino, in one place.

Right now the answers Camino needs live in different tools. Marketing sits in Meta, Instagram, TikTok and YouTube. Trading and labour sit in EPOS. Costs sit with suppliers and in finance. Pulling them together to answer a simple question means exports, spreadsheets and a wait.

Milo brings all of it into one plain-English view. Ask a question the way you'd ask a colleague and get the number back in seconds, with marketing spend read against the covers and revenue it drove, and cost and margin watched across every site.

This is one tool for the whole company, not one bolted to a single team. The same question box serves finance, marketing, operations, GMs and owners, giving each of them the answer that matters to their role.
